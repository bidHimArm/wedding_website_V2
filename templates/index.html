ACTIVITIES

  • Side A - Arts and cultural activities


    • Pay a visit to the MAMA Musée d'Art Moderne et Contemporain , the architecture of the building is astonnishing (2:44)
    • Go checkout some algerian art at the National Museum of Fine Arts of Algiers (2:12)
    • Learn about Algiers history by visiting Le Bastion 23 - Palais des Raïs (2:12)
    • Have a taste of a Pacha's life at Le musée du Bardo (3:47)
    • La Basilique de Notre-Dame d'Afrique is one of the biggest cathedrals of algeria. The view from the Roman Catholic basilica is astonishing
    • The Museum of Popular Arts and Traditions is an art museum located in downtown Algiers. Its displays Algerian art, including rugs, jewellery, pottery and costumes.
    • Cathédrale du Sacré-Cœur d'Alger is a Roman Catholic church located in Algiers, Algeria. Completed in 1956, it became the new cathedral in the capital after the Cathedral of Saint Philip of Algiers reconverted into a Muslim Ketchaoua Mosque.
    • The Ketchaoua Mosque is a mosque built during the Ottoman rule in the 17th century and is located at the foot of the Casbah, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site
